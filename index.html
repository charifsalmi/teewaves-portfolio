About Me

Hi, I’m Charif, a designer who enjoys turning creative ideas into wearable art. I’m all about finding the balance between simplicity and originality, creating designs that stand out without overdoing it. I’m inspired by the everyday moments, the quirky, the clever, and the unexpected, ranging from playful humor to bold, minimalist statements.

This portfolio is packed with concepts I’ve been excited to share, each crafted with care and a little bit of wit.

As part of my journey, I used my modest web development skills to create this portfolio, reflecting my dedication to design and readiness to contribute to Amazon Merch on Demand. These are just a few of the many unique ideas I’m excited to bring forward.

I love experimenting with bold concepts, but always with a clean, thoughtful approach. If you’re into creative, no-nonsense designs, you’re in the right place.

And hey, if not, at least you can say you’ve browsed some cool designs today!
